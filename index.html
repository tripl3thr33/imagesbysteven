ABOUT US

Steven Yamaki is one of Hawaii’s most highly acclaimed professional photographers. His work and talent are recognized throughout the state of Hawaii. His photography has received local and national recognition and awards. With his Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Business Administration, Steven combined his love of photography with his dream of having his own business. He opened his first studio in Pearl City in 1974.

With extensive training at prestigious West Coast School of Photography in California, Steven is a member of Professional Photographers of America, the Professional Photographers of California, and the Professional Photographers of Hawaii.
